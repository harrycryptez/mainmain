How to count your frames & windows

Not sure how many frames you have? Follow these simple steps before booking — it takes about 5 minutes and helps us give you an accurate quote instantly.

1

Find a window opening

Stand outside and look at one window opening in your wall — that's the hole cut into the wall.

2

Count the panes (frames)

Each glass panel inside that opening is one frame. A window divided by a center bar = 2 frames.

3

Walk around the whole house

Go room by room and count every frame on every floor, including basement windows and glass doors.

4

Separate exterior vs interior

Decide if you want outside only, inside only, or both. You'll need that count for each service.

Quick example: A typical Calgary 2-storey home has 20–35 frames. At $7.99/frame exterior that's roughly $160–$280 for a full exterior clean — no surprises.
